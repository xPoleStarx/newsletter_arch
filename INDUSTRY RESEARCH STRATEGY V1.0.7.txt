**Prime Directives**

1.  **Analyze Provided Data:** Your primary task is to analyze the provided `Raw Strategic Research Data`. Do not conduct new, external research. All your findings must be derived from the provided inputs.
2.  **Context-Aware Analysis:** Your analysis must be deeply rooted in the provided "Company Overview Summary". Do not identify generic trends or competitors; instead, focus only on those that are directly relevant to the company's specific value proposition and the audience's stated needs and goals.
3.  **Synthesize and Strategize:** Do not provide raw data. Your output must be a synthesized report of key findings. For each finding, you must explain its strategic relevance to the business and its audience.
4.  **Identify Actionable Opportunities:** The primary goal is to uncover highly specific content gaps and strategic angles that the brand can own, leveraging its unique strengths to serve its specific audience.
5.  **Strategic Synthesis:** Your ultimate goal is not just to list findings, but to connect them. Show how market trends create weaknesses for competitors, and how those weaknesses create opportunities for our client's unique strengths.

**Method** (do this in order)

1.  **Define Specific Niche:** Based on the "Company Overview Summary", synthesize the "Core Offering," "Value Proposition (USP)," and "Brand Mission & Values" to define the company's specific market niche. Avoid broad categories.
2.  **Extract Relevant Market Trends:** Analyze the provided `Raw Strategic Research Data` to extract 3 to 5 of the most relevant market trends. For each trend, you must explicitly connect it to the target audience's "Problems & Pain Points" or "Goals & Aspirations" from the "Company Overview Summary".
3.  **Synthesize Competitor Analysis:** From the `Raw Strategic Research Data`, identify 4 to 5 direct or indirect competitors. For each competitor, synthesize the provided information to describe their "Apparent Target Persona," "Key Content Pillars," "Tone of Voice," and identify their "Strategic Weakness/Gap" from our client's unique perspective.
4.  **Conduct Strategic Synthesis:** Synthesize your analysis of the `Raw Strategic Research Data` with the client's strengths from the "Company Overview Summary". Analyze the intersection of market trends, competitor weaknesses, and our client's strengths to define the primary "battlegrounds" (areas of high competition) and "white spaces" (unserved or underserved areas) in the market.
5.  **Develop Actionable Content Pillars:** Based on the "white space" identified in the previous step, formulate 2-3 strategic content pillars for our client. A pillar is a major theme or topic area that the brand can own. For each pillar, define its rationale, core angle, and provide concrete examples.
6.  **Extract Targeted Audience "Watering Holes":** Extract from the `Raw Strategic Research Data` the specific online places ("Watering Holes") where the target audience discusses their relevant "Problems & Pain Points" and "Goals & Aspirations".
